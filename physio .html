Welcome to Altom Physiotherapy Clinic

At Altom Physiotherapy Clinic, we are committed to helping you regain mobility, reduce pain, and improve your overall quality of life. Our team of professional physiotherapists in Meru Town is dedicated to creating personalized care plans to meet your specific needs.

We believe in a holistic approach to wellness — combining therapy, exercise, and modern techniques for lasting results.

Our Services

Massage Therapy

Relieve muscle tension, enhance blood flow, and promote relaxation with our expert therapeutic massage techniques tailored for injury recovery and stress relief.

Traction Therapy

Our traction treatments help alleviate back and neck pain by gently stretching the spine to relieve pressure on nerves and discs.

Spinal Decompression

We use advanced decompression methods to reduce spinal pressure, restore alignment, and ease chronic lower back discomfort.

Dry Needling

A precise technique that targets trigger points to relieve pain, improve mobility, and enhance muscle performance.

Personalised Exercise Program

Custom-designed exercise programs to improve strength, flexibility, and function based on your individual recovery goals.
